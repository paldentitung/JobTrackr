Frequently Asked Questions
How do I add a new job?

Click the “Add Job” button, fill in the job details, and press “Save.”

Can I edit a job after adding it?

Yes, click the “Edit” icon on the job card, update the details, and save.

How do I mark a job as completed?

Click the checkbox on the job card. The job will move to the “Completed” section.

Is there a way to see more details of a job?

Yes, click the job description to toggle between short and full text.

Can I filter jobs by type or location?

Yes, use the filters at the top of the job list to sort by type, location, or priority.
